A custom keyboard is like a tailored suit, it's an expression of who you are.

Two years ago, when the pandemic first started, the author became interested in playing the guitar. However, while scrolling through Facebook, they stumbled upon a post about customizing keyboards. This piqued their interest and they decided to try it out for themselves. They purchased custom keycaps, switches, and other components to improve the appearance and sound of their keyboard. Despite being busy with other things, the author continued to pursue this hobby for a while. However, due to a lack of time, they eventually stopped customizing keyboards. One reason for this was because their mother kept telling them that one keyboard was enough.

Customizing keyboard is my passion

As a third-year BSIT student at Lyceum of the Philippines Laguna, I have always been interested in the intersection of technology and creativity. That's why my hobby of keyboard customization resonates with me so deeply. At the age of 21, I have been able to combine my technical skills and love for technology with my creative side through this hobby.

Through my blog, I aim to inspire and educate others on the endless possibilities that come with customizing keyboards. From choosing the right keycaps to enhance your typing experience, to adding custom lighting to set the perfect mood, I will guide you through the many aspects of keyboard customization. I'll also share some of my favorite software tweaks and mods, which can help you boost your productivity and elevate your setup to the next level.
